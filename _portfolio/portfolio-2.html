--- title: "Hitomezashi Stitch Pattern Generator" excerpt: " A short program that generates cool patterns
" collection: portfolio --- This is a Numberphile-inspired short programming project that randomly generates a "stitch pattern" composed of horizontal and vertical lines that either begin on a dash or a "hole". For more information about Hitomezashi stitch patterns, I recommend watching Numberphile video that inspired the creation of this program.
